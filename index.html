A little about me

I am a collaborative, self-motivated, and detail-oriented professional, excited to apply problem-solving and analytical skills to a developer position that will fulfill my strive for learning. I am looking for a role where I can not only expand my knowledge and grow professionally but a role where I can work with others, be challenged in an ever-changing environment, take responsibility and ownership of the work I am a part of, and be proud of the work that I have done.

After finding out about programming through a YouTube video and getting my first Arduino project to compile, I was hooked. I fell in love with being able to build something from scratch with some careful planning and problem-solving. Over the course of a few months, I learned everything I could from anywhere I could about programming. I realized that my passion for tinkering on cars and small electronics as well as my natural ability to learn on the fly, absorb information quickly, solve complex problems, and simplify complex projects was very useful in programming. After teaching myself how to build and publish numerous websites and servers, as well as a few mobile apps I decided to enroll in Tech Elevator to turn my passion into a rewarding career.

Elevator has taught me a multitude of skills and has only furthered my passion for software development and fast pace learning. Tech Elevator's focus on technical and collaborative skills gives me the ideal starting point for a career in full-stack and backend development. I am excited to channel my passion and excitement for coding into a rewarding career, and I hope to find a role that can support my drive for success.
